Tradeaux a Corporate Business Flat Bootstrap Responsive Web Template

Tradeaux is a Corporate Business bootstrap template for any business or portfolio website, it’s fully responsive design and retina ready, looks stunning on any device. It features a very clean and professional design that is perfect for showcasing your work, and have tons of layout possibilities with unlimited variations and colors. Template can be used for any type of website: business, corporate, portfolio, products, marketing, etc.

Tradeaux is designed using HTML5 and CSS3, this multiple page flat responsive template has crisp animation and displays your business services like About, Services, Portfolio and a detailed Contact page . Check demo for clear understanding.
